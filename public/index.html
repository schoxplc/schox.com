Welcome Entrepreneurs

In 2003, after working at large firms for five years, Jeffrey Schox founded the Schox Patent Group with the following mission: to build patent portfolios and craft patent strategies that enable startups to increase their valuation and attract funding.

There are several great patent law firms, both large and small, that help startups. But the Schox Patent Group is the only patent law firm that combines the talent and connections of a large firm with the cost structure and efficiencies of a small firm.
